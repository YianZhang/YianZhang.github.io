Hi, I’m Yian Zhang

I am looking for graduate school opportunities in NLP


I am an undergraduate student from NYU Shanghai. My main research interests are training techniques in NLP and neural network probing and analysis. I want to find out what NLP models understand and how to teach them more.

I am advised by Sam Bowman on natural langauge understanding. I am also a member of Machine Learning for Language (ML²) at CILVR.


Aside from NLP, I am proudly advised by Gus Xia on multimodal music learning and I worked with Mohamad Eid on HCI in 2019 Summer.
