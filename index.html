Hi, I’m Yian Zhang,

an NLP and DL enthusiast.


As an MSCS student at Stanford, I am interested in NLP research and using the latest NLP/ML techniques to solve real-world problems. Currently, I am working with Percy Liang to evaluate and demystify foundation models.

Back in college, I was advised by Sam Bowman and worked on language model pretraining and probing. I was also a member of Machine Learning for Language (ML²) at CILVR.


Aside from NLP, I was proudly advised by Gus Xia on multimodal music learning and I worked with Mohamad Eid on HCI in 2019 Summer.
